Our Rescue Mission

Every app I sell helps fund trap-neuter-return programs and care for the cats in my community.

What Is TNR?

Trap-Neuter-Return (TNR) is the humane, effective approach to managing community cat populations. Cats are humanely trapped, spayed or neutered by a veterinarian, and returned to their outdoor home. Over time, this stabilizes and gradually reduces colony sizes.

How We Help

Funding TNR

A portion of every app sale goes directly to local TNR clinics and the volunteers who do the hard work of trapping, transporting, and caring for community cats.

Supplies & Care

I donate traps, food, shelters, and medical supplies to community cat caregivers. These essentials keep cats safe and healthy while they live outdoors.

Awareness

I try to spread the word about humane cat management. TNR works — and more people need to know about it.
