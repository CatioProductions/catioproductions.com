MagnaDesk

A digital corkboard that lives inside your Windows wallpaper. Sticky notes, images, drawings, and live widgets — baked right into your desktop.

Download See How It Works

What Is MagnaDesk?

MagnaDesk places interactive sticky notes, images, drawings, and live widgets directly into your Windows wallpaper. Items are rendered into the wallpaper bitmap itself — there's no visible overlay window in normal use. They sit below your desktop icons, behave like part of the wallpaper, and use zero system resources when you're not editing.

When you need to add or edit items, press Ctrl+Shift+M to open Edit Mode — a transparent overlay spans all your monitors with a full editing toolbar. Make your changes, press Esc, and everything bakes back into the wallpaper.

Two Simple States

Viewing Mode

Your items are baked into the wallpaper. No overlay window, no running process drawing on your screen. Items sit below desktop icons like they're part of the wallpaper — because they are. Zero resource overhead.

Edit Mode

Press Ctrl+Shift+M or click the tray icon. A transparent overlay appears across all monitors with an editing toolbar. Add, move, resize, rotate, and delete items. Press Esc to bake everything back into the wallpaper.

What You Can Put on Your Desktop

Sticky Notes

Editable text notes in five colors — yellow, pink, mint, blue, and white. Move, resize, and rotate them anywhere on your desktop.

Images

Place any JPG or PNG on your desktop with crop support. Or use Win+Shift+S to snip anything on screen, then Ctrl+V to paste it — web pages, spreadsheets, emails, anything visible.

Freehand Drawing

Draw directly on your desktop with the pen tool. Five colors, adjustable stroke width (1–12px), and a canvas size slider so you can write large and shrink the result. Eraser tool removes individual strokes.

Live Widgets

Widgets display live data from your Microsoft and Google accounts, rendered right into your wallpaper. Connect multiple accounts and each widget can pull from a specific account or aggregate across all of them.

Task List

Display tasks from a single Microsoft To Do or Google Tasks list. Checkboxes, due dates, and completion status — all visible at a glance on your desktop.

To-Do List

Aggregates tasks across all your lists, grouped by due date — Overdue, Today, Tomorrow, Upcoming, and No Date. Like the To Do app's smart view, but on your wallpaper.

Calendar

Upcoming events grouped by day with subject, time, and location. Shows 7 days ahead by default. Works with Outlook and Google Calendar.

OneNote

Your most recently modified OneNote pages with notebook, section, and relative timestamps. Quick visual of what you've been working on.

Mail

Unread messages from your inbox with sender, subject, and preview. Red indicator for high-importance messages. Shows "Inbox zero" when you're caught up.

Widget Features

  • Connect multiple Microsoft and Google accounts simultaneously
  • Each widget can show data from one account or all accounts combined
  • 11 background color themes — Dark, Slate, Navy, Cobalt, Teal, Forest, Olive, Rust, Wine, Plum, Charcoal
  • Resizing reveals more or fewer items — text stays readable, never scales
  • Auto-refresh on a configurable interval, plus manual refresh

Built-In Wallpaper Engine

MagnaDesk doesn't just draw on your wallpaper — it manages it too.

Single Image

Set any image file as your wallpaper with your choice of fit — Stretch, Fill, Fit (letterbox), Center, or Tile.

Slideshow

Cycle through images in a folder at a configurable interval. Sequential or shuffle. Advance anytime with Ctrl+Alt+W or from the right-click desktop menu.

Solid Color

Pick a preset color or enter a hex value for a clean, minimal background.

Web Image Sources

Search and download wallpapers from Unsplash, Pexels, and Wallhaven directly in the app. Uses your own free API keys — they never leave your machine.

Editing Tools

The editing toolbar floats at the bottom of your screen. Drag it anywhere with the grip handle.

  • Select (S) — click to select items. Drag to move. Corner handles to resize, rotation handle to rotate, × to delete
  • Add Note (N) — creates a new sticky note at the center of your primary monitor
  • Add Image (I) — opens a file picker to place an image on the canvas
  • Paste (Ctrl+V) — pastes a clipboard image. Use Win+Shift+S to capture anything on screen first
  • Pen (P) — freehand drawing with adjustable stroke width and canvas size
  • Eraser (E) — click a stroke to erase it
  • Undo (Ctrl+Z) — reverses the last action
  • Done (Esc) — exits Edit Mode and bakes everything into the wallpaper

Multi-Monitor Support

The MagnaDesk canvas spans all connected monitors. Place items on any screen and they'll be baked into the correct position in the wallpaper.

  • Items on disconnected monitors automatically migrate to the nearest active monitor
  • When the monitor reconnects, items return to their original positions
  • Use "Recover Off-Screen Items" from the tray menu if anything gets lost

Account Integration

Microsoft

Sign in using the device code flow — you'll get a code to enter at microsoft.com/devicelogin. Grants access to To Do, Calendar, OneNote, and Mail. Works with both personal and work/school accounts.

Google

Sign in via browser-based OAuth. Grants access to Google Calendar, Gmail, and Google Tasks. Multiple accounts supported.

All tokens are encrypted via Windows DPAPI and stored locally. Nothing leaves your machine.

Requirements
