About Catio Productions

Catio Productions is just me — one indie dev with a few AI co-pilots and a house full of cats who supervise every coding session. I build Windows apps that I actually want to use, and the AIs help me ship them faster than any one person should be able to.

But the real reason behind all of this? The cats. I became involved with some strays in my neighborhood, trying to get them socialized and adopted into good homes. What I thought would be a temporary gesture of goodwill toward some stray animals — something that would also help the neighborhood — turned me into a permanent colony manager. I'm passionate about helping community cats through trap-neuter-return (TNR) programs. A portion of every app sale goes directly toward TNR efforts in my area — funding spay/neuter surgeries, buying traps and supplies, and supporting the volunteers who do this work every day.

So yeah — it's me, some AIs, and a lot of cats. If you use Windows and care about kitties, you're in the right place.
